About the Game
Twisted Wonderland is a Japanese Villains Academy adventure and card gacha game for mobile, created by Disney Japan and published by Aniplex. Characters inspired by Disney villains from various franchises weave wicked schoolyard schemes in this magical adventure featuring rhythm games and RPG-style battles. Concept, main story, and character designs are by Yana Toboso (Original creator of "Black Butler").
A deliciously dark and enchanting drama awaits you.
