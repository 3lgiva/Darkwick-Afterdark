What is This Server About?

This server is dedicated to Tokyo Debunker, a (Visual Novel / RPG / Adventure Game) set in a modern-day Tokyo where supernatural phenomena, known as "Distortions," plague the city. As a Debunker, you possess the unique ability to perceive and interact with these hidden threats. Your mission: to expose the truth, protect the innocent, and prevent the city from succumbing to the encroaching darkness.

  • Unravel Mysteries: Discuss gripping narratives and moral dilemmas with others.
  • Confront Distortions: Share strategies for battling malevolent entities.
  • Forge Bonds: Connect with a diverse community of players and fans.
  • Shape Your Destiny: Debate choices and explore branching storylines together.
