Stigma & Abilities

Analeigh's Stigma is "Fortuna",This allows her to slightly sway her own or another individual’s luck

  • Note: She regularly uses her stigma to win in the casino despite the drawback. Her thought process is "it’s not cheating if you don't get caught”, as well as her somewhat enjoying the painful consequences.
  • Pros: Winner winner chicken dinner
  • Cons: Each time she boosts her own or other’s luck, the universe reacts by inflicting a random physical consequence on her body—severity and form vary unpredictably. This can range from nose bleeds to actual broken bones. She once even passed out mid-card game due to her careless stigma usage.
